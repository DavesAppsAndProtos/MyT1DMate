# My T1D Mate

A companion app for managing Type 1 Diabetes — live glucose tracking, dose calculation, and more, with Android Auto support for hands-free dashboard glucose readings.

## Features

- **Live glucose tracking** — connects to LibreLinkUp and shows your current reading, trend, and history graph
- **Dose Calc** — quick dose calculation support
- **Hold That Thought** — pin notes for later
- **Weight Tracker** — track weight alongside your glucose data
- **Android Auto** — see your glucose reading on your car's dashboard, hands-free (requires [My T1D Mate Auto](https://github.com/DavesAppsAndProtos/MyT1DMateAuto))
- **Shape This App** — direct feedback channel to influence what gets built next

## Coming soon

Exercise tracking, Morning routine, Reports, Adaptive polling, Chat (premium), Notifications, Accessibility, and a return of Customise Dashboard — see the in-app "Coming Soon" menu for the live list.

## Requirements

- Android device
- A LibreLinkUp account with an active FreeStyle Libre sensor

## Installation

1. Download the latest APK from [Releases](../../releases)
2. Install on your Android phone (you may need to allow installs from unknown sources)
3. Follow the in-app onboarding to connect your LibreLinkUp account

## For reference only

My T1D Mate is a glanceable companion display. Do not adjust treatment based on the app display alone — always refer to your official LibreLink app and clinical guidance for treatment decisions.
